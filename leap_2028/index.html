Our 3-Year Climate Action Plan

A comprehensive roadmap to reduce emissions, build resilience, and create a sustainable future for our community

Explore the Plan Get Involved

Plan Overview

Our ambitious 3-year climate plan focuses on measurable action across key sectors to achieve meaningful emissions reductions and build community resilience.

Energy Transition

Accelerate the shift to renewable energy sources and improve energy efficiency across all sectors.

Sustainable Transport

Transform transportation systems with electric vehicles, public transit, and active mobility options.

Nature-Based Solutions

Leverage natural ecosystems for carbon sequestration, biodiversity protection, and climate adaptation.

3-Year Timeline

A phased approach to achieving our climate goals

Year 1: Foundation

  • Baseline emissions assessment
  • Policy framework development
  • Community engagement programs
  • Initial renewable energy projects

Year 2: Implementation

  • Large-scale renewable deployment
  • Electric vehicle infrastructure
  • Green building standards
  • Ecosystem restoration projects

Year 3: Acceleration

  • System-wide integration
  • Innovation hub development
  • Regional collaboration
  • Long-term strategy planning

Key Initiatives

Strategic programs driving our climate action forward

Clean Energy Transition

Deploy 500MW of renewable energy capacity and retrofit 10,000 buildings for energy efficiency.

Sustainable Mobility

EV charging stations 1,000 target

Carbon Sequestration

Plant 1 million trees and restore 5,000 hectares of natural habitats for carbon capture.

Trees planted 1M target

Circular Economy

Achieve 75% waste diversion from landfills and establish regional recycling hubs.

Waste diversion 75% target
